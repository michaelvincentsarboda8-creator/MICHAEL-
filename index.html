About Us

Discover our rich history and commitment to excellence

Our History

Binalbagan Catholic College has been a beacon of educational excellence in the community for decades. Founded with a vision to provide quality Catholic education, we have nurtured generations of students who have gone on to become leaders in their respective fields.

Our Mission

To provide holistic Catholic education that develops morally upright, academically excellent, and socially responsible individuals.

Our Vision

To be a leading Catholic educational institution recognized for excellence in academics, character formation, and community service.
